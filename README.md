The Mathematics Behind Modern AI

Code, notebooks, exercises, and figures for the book The Mathematics Behind Modern AI.

A concept-first, code-supported journey into the mathematical foundations of artificial intelligence.
---

## Philosophy

> This is not a book about mathematics for its own sake.
> It is a book about understanding intelligence through mathematics.

> Meaning is geometry.
> Geometry is numbers.
> Therefore, meaning is numbers.

---

## What This Repository Provides

For each chapter, you will find:

* Conceptual explanations (in the book)
* NumPy notebooks (build intuition)
* PyTorch notebooks (real-world AI)
* Exercises (test understanding)
* Solutions (guided answers)
* Visualizations (geometric insight)
